Some Background

Jack is a mix of Dachshund, Golden Retriever and Terrier. He can't seem to get fetch, or any other kind of games besides 'Attack the Hand' or 'Chase the Feet.' Jack has lived with us for over two years, and he is eight years old.

If you want to find a dog like Jack, or support the shelter that held onto him, visit The Oregon Humane Society.
